subject Body & Paragraphs

Body Large (16px)

Lucid leverages white space as a structural element. Paragraphs should maintain a line-height of 1.6 to ensure readability in data-heavy environments or long-form documentation.

Body Medium (14px)

Secondary body text uses on-surface-variant to create a natural hierarchy. This is the workhorse of our dashboard for metadata and supporting content.
